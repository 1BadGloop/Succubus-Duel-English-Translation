When this card is Normal Summoned: Inflict [Lock (2 Turns)] on all cards on the opponent's field. Cards with [Lock] cannot activate their effects; however; effects already activated are not negated.\n[2]. Once per turn; when a card other than this one inflicts [Lock]: Release that [Lock] and draw two cards.
